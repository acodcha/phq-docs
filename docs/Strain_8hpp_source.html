65 /// \brief Three-dimensional Euclidean strain symmetric dyadic tensor. Contains six components in
66 /// Cartesian coordinates: xx, xy = yx, xz = zx, yy, yz = zy, and zz. For the scalar components or
67 /// resultants of a strain tensor, see PhQ::ScalarStrain. For the time rate of change of strain, see
68 /// PhQ::StrainRate, PhQ::Time, and PhQ::Frequency.
