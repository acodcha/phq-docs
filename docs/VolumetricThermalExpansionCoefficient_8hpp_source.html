41 /// \brief Volumetric thermal expansion coefficient. Not to be confused with the linear thermal
42 /// expansion coefficient; see PhQ::LinearThermalExpansionCoefficient. For isotropic materials, the
43 /// volumetric thermal expansion coefficient is usually three times the linear thermal expansion
44 /// coefficient.
