template<typename NumericType = double>
class PhQ::ScalarVelocityGradient< NumericType >

Scalar component or resultant of a three-dimensional Euclidean velocity gradient dyadic tensor. For the related tensor, see PhQ::VelocityGradient. Can also represent the time rate of change of a scalar displacement gradient; see PhQ::ScalarDisplacementGradient, PhQ::Time, and PhQ::Frequency.
