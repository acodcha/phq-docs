39 /// \brief P-wave modulus of elasticity of a deformable solid material. A measure of a deformable
40 /// solid material's elastic modulus. For other measures of a material's elastic modulus, see
41 /// PhQ::YoungModulus, PhQ::ShearModulus, PhQ::IsentropicBulkModulus, PhQ::IsothermalBulkModulus,
42 /// PhQ::LameModulus, and PhQ::PoissonRatio.
