70 /// \brief Surface area or cross-sectional area. Can also represent a scalar component of a vector
71 /// area or the magnitude of a vector area. Any closed surface has a vector area: it is the surface
72 /// integral of its surface normal direction. A vector area is an oriented area; it is the
73 /// three-dimensional Euclidean vector representation of an area; see PhQ::VectorArea.
