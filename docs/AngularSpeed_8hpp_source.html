46 /// @brief Angular speed, also known as angular rate or angular frequency. Represents the time rate
47 /// of change of an angle; see also PhQ::Angle, PhQ::ScalarAngularAcceleration, PhQ::Time, and
48 /// PhQ::Frequency. Can also represent a component or the magnitude of an angular velocity
49 /// pseudovector.
50 template <typename Number = double>

52 public:
