41 /// \brief Linear thermal expansion coefficient. Not to be confused with the volumetric thermal
42 /// expansion coefficient; see PhQ::VolumetricThermalExpansionCoefficient. For isotropic materials,
43 /// the volumetric thermal expansion coefficient is usually three times the linear thermal expansion
44 /// coefficient.
