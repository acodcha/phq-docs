38 /// \brief Poisson's ratio of a deformable solid material. Measures the deformation of a deformable
39 /// solid material subjected to a load in directions perpendicular to the direction of loading.
40 /// Poisson's ratios range from -1 to 0.5, though most deformable solid materials have a
41 /// Poisson's ratio between 0 and 0.5.
42 template <typename NumericType = double>
