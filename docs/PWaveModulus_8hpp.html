class  PhQ::PWaveModulus< NumericType >
 P-wave modulus of elasticity of a deformable solid material. A measure of a deformable solid material's elastic modulus. For other measures of a material's elastic modulus, see PhQ::YoungModulus, PhQ::ShearModulus, PhQ::IsentropicBulkModulus, PhQ::IsothermalBulkModulus, PhQ::LameModulus, and PhQ::PoissonRatio.
